Our Story

Shri Sai Dwarkamai Vridhashram was founded in 2011 with a simple yet powerful vision — to create a home where elderly individuals can live their lives with dignity, love, and care.

What started as a small shelter for 10 elders has grown into a vibrant community serving over 500 senior citizens. Inspired by the compassion of Sai Baba, our founder believed that no elder should ever feel abandoned or alone.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities to include medical care, recreational activities, spiritual programs, and counseling — all aimed at ensuring our residents lead fulfilling lives in their golden years.

Vision & Mission

🎞

Our Vision

A world where every senior citizen is valued, respected, and lives with dignity — never feeling abandoned or forgotten. We envision a society that cherishes its elders as the pillars of wisdom and love.

❤

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and joyful home for elderly individuals by offering comprehensive care including healthcare, nutrition, recreation, spiritual fulfillment, and emotional support.
