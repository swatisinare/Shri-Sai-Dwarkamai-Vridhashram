Become a Volunteer

Your time and compassion can change an elder's day

Why Volunteer With Us?

Volunteering at Dwarkamai is more than service — it's a life-changing experience. You'll bring smiles, companionship, and purpose to our elders while gaining invaluable life lessons in return.

Roles Available

Choose a role that matches your skills and interests

🧡

Caretaker

Spend time with residents, assist with daily activities, and provide companionship and emotional support.

🎆

Event Organizer

Help plan and organize festivals, cultural events, birthday celebrations, and recreational activities.

☤

Medical Volunteer

Assist with health checkups, medicine distribution, and physiotherapy sessions (medical background preferred).

📚

Teaching & Mentoring

Conduct workshops, teach digital literacy, read to residents, or share skills like painting and music.

💼

Administrative Support

Help with documentation, fundraising, social media management, and day-to-day operational tasks.
